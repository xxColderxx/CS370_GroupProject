How do you play the game?


As a player, you will control the object using your arrow keyboard.
↑ : for moving up
↓ : for moving down
←: for moving left
→: for moving right


You as a player has to catch the blue ball while avoid touching the red ball. If you catch the blue ball, you get receive a points, but if you catch the red ball, you will lose the same amount of points. The duration of each game will be 30 seconds.

The score:
Before the player starts playing, the player can choose the difficulty.

  • In easy mode: The balls are moving relatively slowly so you had an easier time to catch it. Every time you catch the blue ball you will score 10 points. For catching the red ball, you will lose 10 points.
  • In medium mode: The balls will move faster than the easy mode. Every time you catch the blue ball you will score 15 points. For catching the red ball, you will lose 15 points.
  • In hard mode: The balls will fly quicker. Every time you catch the blue ball you will score 20 points. For catching the red ball, you will lose 20 points.
  • In hell mode: The balls will be flying lighting quick. Every time you catch the monster you will score 30 points. For catching the red ball, you will lose 30 points.
